# Medical Guidelines for NeuroHealth Prototype

## General Nutrition
For sustainable weight loss, aim for a modest calorie deficit of 300-500 calories per day. Incorporate lean proteins, vegetables, and complex carbohydrates. 

## Dietary Restrictions
- **Vegetarian**: Ensure adequate intake of Vitamin B12, Iron, and Zinc. Good plant-based protein sources include lentils, tofu, quinoa, and chickpeas.
- **Keto**: Focus on high healthy fats (avocados, nuts) and very low carbohydrates.

## Exercise and Joint Pain
- **Knee Pain**: Avoid high-impact exercises like running or jumping. Substitute with low-impact options such as swimming, cycling, or elliptical trainers. Strength training for the quadriceps and hamstrings can provide better support for the knee joint.
- **Lower Back Pain**: Focus on core strengthening exercises. Avoid heavy lifting or twisting motions. Walking and swimming are highly recommended.

## Sleep Hygiene
Adults require 7-9 hours of sleep. Maintain a consistent sleep schedule and minimize screen time 1 hour before bed.

## Hydration
Drink at least 2 liters (about 8 glasses) of water per day, increasing intake during exercise or hot weather.
